💻 Research Projects

🔐 Project 1: CRYSTALS-Kyber NTT Hardware Accelerator

Problem: CRYSTALS-Kyber requires high-performance NTT operations computationally intensive on general-purpose processors.

Solution: 4-parallel NTT architecture with banked memory and pipelined Barrett reduction.

Results: 245 MHz | 1.28 Gbps | 2,845 LUTs | 12.4 μs Key Generation
